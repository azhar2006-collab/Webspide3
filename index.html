Our Story

Where Wellness
Finds its Rhythm

Rhythm Divine was born from a vision to create a yoga and wellness sanctuary specifically designed for the unique needs of women in their 40s and beyond — and for the next generation of young practitioners from age 10.

Nestled in the heart of Anna Nagar West, our studio offers a nurturing, non-competitive environment where every session is lovingly tailored to your body's wisdom and life stage.

With online classes extending our reach beyond studio walls, your wellness journey knows no boundaries.
