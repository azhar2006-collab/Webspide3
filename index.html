Our Story

Where Wellness
Finds its Rhythm

Rhythm Divine was born from a vision to create a yoga and wellness sanctuary specifically designed for the unique needs of women in their 40s and beyond — and for the next generation of young practitioners from age 10.

Nestled in Anna Nagar West, Chennai, our studio offers a nurturing, non-competitive environment where every breath, every posture, and every session is tailored to your body's wisdom.

With online classes extending our reach beyond the studio walls, your wellness journey knows no boundaries.
